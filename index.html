Post-doc | UC Davis Center for Population Biology


RESEARCH INTERESTS I am a population biologist using mathematics to investigate how ecological and genetic factors influence the evolutionary process, and vice versa. My primary interests are evolutionary rescue, the effects of genetic backgrounds and linkage, and metabolic scaling theory. I am now building methods to infer past eco-evolutionary dynamics (esp. rescue) from genomic data.
