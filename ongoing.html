Evolution affects fisheries projections under climate change.
With Joey Bernhardt and William Cheung.
We're using metabolic theory and evolving integral projection models to predict how fish body size distributions and population growth rates are expected to change with warming oceans.

Evolutionary tipping points.
With Olivier Cotto, Sally Otto, and Ophélie Ronce.
We're investigating when and why evolutionary tipping points arise in stage-structured models, and where else they might appear.

Extreme events: does evolution help or hurt?
With Kelsey Lyberger and Sebastian Schreiber.
How much will populations evolve during extreme events (eg hurricanes, drought)? How does this affect persistence? We're building some simple quantitative genetics models that suggest evolution is not always helpful.
