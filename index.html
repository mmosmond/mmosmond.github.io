RESEARCH INTERESTS

I am an evolutionary theoretician investigating how ecological and genetic factors influence the evolutionary process, and vice versa. My primary interests are evolutionary rescue, the effect of genetic backgrounds and linkage, and metabolic scaling theory. I am now building methods to infer past eco-evolutionary dynamics (esp. rescue) from extant genomic data.
